If you don't know the what type of Exceptions will come then used Exception e which is like super class or father of all Exception.
Types of Exceptions:

1-IOEXCEPTIONS
2-SQLEXCEPTIONS
3-CLASSNOTFOUNDEXCEPTIONS
The above three are called the checked Exceptions.

4-RUNTIME Exceptions
Runtime Exceptions are :
a. Arithmetic Exceptions
b. NullPointerExceptions
c. NumberFormatExceptions
d. IndexOutOfBoundsExceptions: ArrayIndexOutOfBoundExceptions and StringIndexOutOfBoundExceptions.

Keywords to remember for Exceptions:
Try
Catch
Finally
Throw
Throws
